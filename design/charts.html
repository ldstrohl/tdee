Project TDEE — Chart Designs v3

Interactive, 1 year of example data through the real engine math. Prediction is now an independent overlay — it keeps your selected range and only adds the forward projection.

1 · Trend Graph

Raw weight 14-day EMA Goal Goal-pace projection Current-pace projection

What it's for: raw points vs the smoothed 14-day trend. The Prediction overlay keeps whatever range you've selected and adds two dashed forward projections to the goal: goal pace (green) and your current measured pace (blue), each dated.

How it works: trend = EMA, α=2/15. Goal-pace uses your chosen rate (0.5 lb/wk → Jul 21, 2026); current-pace uses your real recent slope (→ Aug 24, 2026). Range pills set history shown; Prediction only changes the forward part, not the range.

2 · Expenditure Graph

Calories eaten Computed TDEE Deficit (under TDEE)

What it's for: intake bars vs the moving TDEE line; the gap is shaded green only when you're under TDEE. Bars above the line are an obvious surplus.

How it works: TDEE = avg intake − (trend Δ × 3500/window), formula-seeded only for the first ~14 days.
